Detailed Description

This module allows patients in Synthea to lose weight.

It will be triggered when patients hit a specified age and weight threshold. At that point, patients may chose to manage their weight. If they do, it will suspend any weight adjustments from the LifecycleModule. Patients will then lose weight based on adherence generated by this module. Given successful weight loss, the patients have the chance to later regain all of their weight (very likely given the default probabilities).

Change the BMI vector to reflect successful management of weight.

This will add a new point at the end of the trajectory that will result in the BMI percentile change selected for the person. The change will take place 12 months after the current tail of the trajectory. If that will take the person past 20 years old, the time frame will be cut short to end at 240 months of age.

Determines whether a person meets the thresholds for starting weight management.

With the default settings: Children under 5 do not ever meet the threshold. Patients from ages 5 to 20 meet the threshold if their BMI is at or over the 95th percentile for their age in months Patients 20 and older meet the threshold if their BMI is 30 or over.
