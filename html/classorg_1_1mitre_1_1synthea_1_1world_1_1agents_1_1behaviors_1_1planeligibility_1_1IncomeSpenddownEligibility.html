Detailed Description

A class that defines the elgibility logic for Income medical cost spenddowns (MNIL, in the case of Medicaid).

MNIL allows people who don't qualify for income-based Medicaid to qualify if their expenses bring them down to a certain income bracket. By-age and by-state Standard Medicaid MNIL data from: https://www.medicaidplanningassistance.org/medically-needy-pathway/
