Detailed Description

Configuration that drives TypeOfCare transitions in Synthea.

The concept is that an individual will seek a particular type of care (telemedicine vs. something in person) based on the time in the simulation and their insurance. Some insurance types, or lack thereof will have higher utilization of the emergency department.
