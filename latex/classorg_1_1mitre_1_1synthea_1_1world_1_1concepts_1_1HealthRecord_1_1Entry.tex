\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
All things within a \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1world_1_1concepts_1_1HealthRecord}{Health\+Record}} are instances of \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1world_1_1concepts_1_1HealthRecord_1_1Entry}{Entry}}. 

For example, Observations, Reports, Medications, etc. All Entries have a name, start and stop times, a type, and a list of associated codes.
