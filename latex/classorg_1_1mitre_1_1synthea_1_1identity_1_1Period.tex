\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A simple representation of a time period with a start and end date. 

End date may be null for usage with Seeds to represent an open-\/ended range, if it is the last \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1identity_1_1Seed}{Seed}} for an \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1identity_1_1Entity}{Entity}}.
