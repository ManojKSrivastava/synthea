\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A default implementation of the \mbox{\hyperlink{interfaceorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1helpers_1_1RandomNumberGenerator}{Random\+Number\+Generator}} interface. 

The goal is to isolate sources of randomness by consolidating the use of java.\+util.\+Random or other sources of randomness for traceability.
