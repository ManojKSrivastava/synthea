\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A representation of demographic information for an \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1identity_1_1Entity}{Entity}} for a period of time. 

A seed is considered \char`\"{}ground truth\char`\"{} for the simulation. The city and state supplied in the seed are what Synthea will use determine location, which will impact the providers used. 

Seeds have an associated \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1identity_1_1Period}{Period}}, which is the time range that they should be used for in the simulation. The last seed in a \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1identity_1_1Entity}{Entity}} should have an open ended \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1identity_1_1Period}{Period}}. That is, the \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1identity_1_1Period}{Period}} should have an end date set to null. This means that the seed will be used from its start date until the end of the simulation. 

Seeds may have one or more Variants. These variants can be used to capture error or other deviation from the demographic information in the seeds.
