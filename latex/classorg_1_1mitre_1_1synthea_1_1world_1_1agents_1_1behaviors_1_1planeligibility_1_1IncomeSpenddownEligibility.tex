\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class that defines the elgibility logic for Income medical cost spenddowns (MNIL, in the case of Medicaid). 

MNIL allows people who don\textquotesingle{}t qualify for income-\/based Medicaid to qualify if their expenses bring them down to a certain income bracket. By-\/age and by-\/state Standard Medicaid MNIL data from\+: \href{https://www.medicaidplanningassistance.org/medically-needy-pathway/}{\texttt{ https\+://www.\+medicaidplanningassistance.\+org/medically-\/needy-\/pathway/}}
