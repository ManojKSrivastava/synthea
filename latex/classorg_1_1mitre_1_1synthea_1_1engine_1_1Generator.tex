The returned person will be alive at the end of the simulation. This means that if in the course of the simulation the person dies, a new person will be started to replace them. Note also that if the person dies, the seed to produce them can\textquotesingle{}t be re-\/used (otherwise the new person would die as well) so a new seed is picked, based on the given seed.
