\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A model of employment / unemployment that is expected to be run as a part of the Lifecycle\+Module. 

The model is created with a probability of unemployment. This is likely to come from the SDoH information that is loaded from Location. If a person is employed, every time check\+Employment is called, there is a chance the person becomes unemployed.
