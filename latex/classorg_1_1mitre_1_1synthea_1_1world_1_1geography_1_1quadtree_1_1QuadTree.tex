\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Simple \mbox{\hyperlink{classorg_1_1mitre_1_1synthea_1_1world_1_1geography_1_1quadtree_1_1QuadTree}{Quad\+Tree}} class that is optimized for query speed. 

The query is simplified, because it only takes into account Euclidean distance and does not take into account the elevation, altitude, curvature of the Earth, or features such as roads, mountains, or bodies of water.
